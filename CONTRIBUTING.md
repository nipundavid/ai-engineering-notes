## 🤝 Contributing

Contributions are welcome!

If you find an error, spot outdated information, or have a better way to explain a concept, feel free to:

- Open an issue for discussions or corrections.
- Submit a pull request with improvements.
- Suggest new topics or engineering patterns that would benefit the community.
- Improve diagrams, examples, or references.

### Contribution Guidelines

Before submitting a pull request, please ensure that your contribution:

- Keeps the content practical and engineering-focused.
- Prioritizes official documentation and reliable references over opinion.
- Maintains the existing writing style and repository structure.
- Includes diagrams or examples only when they improve understanding.
- Is generic enough to avoid sharing proprietary or confidential information.

Every contribution—whether it's fixing a typo, improving an explanation, or adding a new engineering note—is appreciated.

Thank you for helping make **AI Engineering Notes** a valuable resource for the AI engineering community.
